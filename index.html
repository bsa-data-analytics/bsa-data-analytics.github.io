Introduction to Data Analytics

Decision making in business is key to success, wheather it is reacting to competitors, customers, the enviroment or making the most out of an opportunity. Making those decisions has become more time critical to a business, the information needed to make those decisions must be available at the right time. This section introduces you to the concepts associated with Data Analytics.

Excel has extensive functions, charts, and statistical analysis capabilities. You will be introduced to some of these tools in these labs.
Advanced Data Analytics

Data Analytics on large sets of structured data requires advanced methods and techniques provided by Data Mining. These methods allow for classfication, prediction and clustering of the data. Analytics on large sets of unstructured data (Big Data) requires specialised infrastructure to store and process.
